Monitask features

  • Automatic Timesheets

    Forget about manual time entries and Excel templates with the accurate, automated time tracking app, available across multiple operating systems.

  • Internet and App Monitoring

    Review websites visited and applications used by your team. Identify unproductive sites and time spent on repetitive tasks.

  • Project Management

    Create and assign projects to responsible employees, review project reports, and track money spent on each project.

Monitask features

  • Offline Time Entry

    No internet connection? No problem. Monitask’s time tracker records time even when an employee is offline. Once an internet connection is available, all recorded time is securely uploaded for your convenience.

  • Screenshots

    Proof-of-work screenshots are a great way to review daily operations and identify unproductive employees. Screenshots can be turned off or blurred for sensitive content.

  • Activity Levels

    Activity levels show how active an employee is during their workday. Activity levels are calculated based on general keyboard and mouse usage.

Monitask features

  • Idle Time Detection

    Automatically identify and remove idle time by enabling this feature. Idle time detection can help you save thousands of dollars a year by reducing your payroll.

  • Employee App

    The Monitask application is for employees of all levels, including contractors and freelancers. This app will enable them to track their work hours for projects assigned to them in Monitask.
